Our Services

Tiling (Commercial & Residential)

At Biggy Monie Construction, we proudly offer expert tiling services tailored to both commercial and residential spaces. Whether you're updating your home with a stylish new look or outfitting a large-scale commercial property, our skilled team is equipped to handle projects of all sizes and styles. We specialize in everything from sleek, contemporary tile installations to timeless, classic finishes that bring warmth and character to any space. Our commitment to excellence means that every tile we lay is done with the utmost precision and care. We use high-quality materials and state-of-the-art techniques to ensure long-lasting durability and flawless results. From floors and walls to intricate backsplashes and feature accents, our tiling solutions are designed to enhance the beauty and functionality of your environment. With a keen eye for design and a dedication to customer satisfaction, we work closely with clients to bring their vision to life—creating spaces that are not only beautiful but also built to stand the test of time.

Swimming Pool Construction

Experience the ultimate in luxury with our custom swimming pool construction services. At Biggy Monie Construction, we specialize in designing and building stunning, high-quality pools that are perfectly tailored to meet your unique style, space, and specific needs. Whether you're dreaming of a serene, private oasis in your own backyard or creating a captivating commercial attraction that will leave a lasting impression, we bring your vision to life with unparalleled craftsmanship. POP Ceiling

Enhance the elegance and ambiance of your interiors with our modern POP (Plaster of Paris) ceiling designs, expertly crafted to transform any space with style, sophistication, and precision. At Biggy Monie Construction, we bring creativity and innovation together to deliver ceiling solutions that not only elevate the visual appeal of your rooms but also reflect contemporary trends and timeless charm. Our team of skilled artisans and designers works meticulously to create POP ceiling designs that suit a wide range of interior styles—whether you're aiming for a minimalist modern look, a luxurious decorative pattern, or a bold architectural statement. We offer a diverse selection of design options including recessed lighting effects, layered tray ceilings, elegant moldings, and intricate patterns that add depth and character to any room. Every POP ceiling we install is carefully measured, designed, and executed to ensure flawless alignment, smooth finishes, and long-lasting durability. We use only top-grade materials and advanced techniques, guaranteeing a result that is both beautiful and built to endure. Whether you're updating a single room, renovating an entire home, or outfitting a commercial property, our modern POP ceiling solutions provide the perfect finishing touch—bringing harmony, elegance, and a touch of luxury to your living or working space.
